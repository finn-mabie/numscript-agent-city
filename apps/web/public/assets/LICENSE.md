**CC0 1.0 Universal**. No attribution is required, but acknowledged here as
good practice.

- `tiny-town/*` — from "Tiny Town" pack

terms per Kenney's license.

If placeholder sprites are present instead of Kenney's, the LICENSE still applies
to the replacement artwork on future asset upgrades.
